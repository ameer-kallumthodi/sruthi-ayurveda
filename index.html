About the Doctor

Holistic Healing Rooted in Ancient Wisdom

Classical Ayurveda Whole-Person Care Online Consultations Diet & Lifestyle Guidance Stress Management

I am an Ayurveda doctor passionate about helping people restore health naturally by correcting digestion, lifestyle, and daily habits. My treatment approach is gentle, practical, and rooted in classical Ayurveda principles.

I focus on understanding each patient as a whole rather than treating isolated symptoms. Along with medicines, I guide patients on diet, routine, and stress management for long-term wellbeing.

Online consultations are available with proper assessment and follow-up care — bringing authentic Ayurveda to your doorstep, wherever you are.
